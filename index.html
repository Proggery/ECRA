What is the ECRA?

ECRA is a bottom up initative launched in 2011 by EU commission, and focusing on theobjective to bring together, expand and optimize European expertise in climate research bysharing existing national research capacities and infrastructures.

Members

ECRA's member institutions are from nine European countries.
